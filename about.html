About David

David is the photographer and videographer of Donkor Reyalis, aka Reyalis. Back in middle school he was tasked with creating a author name for a writting contest, leading him to find Donkor and Reyalis. Donkor meaning "slave" and Reyalis meaning "realism", as well as "realistic". Choosing these words for their meaning and to substitute his name while keeping his initials, bringing the final definition to "slave of realism".
